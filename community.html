How the network compounds

Every ecosystem adds to the whole.

A member who arrives through an event can later help a business owner. A customer of one website can discover another creator. Public identity and contributions can travel now; private relationships stay in their origin until the person chooses to link accounts.

  1. 1
    One network identityA public profile can appear across connected ecosystems with its origin shown. Account linking is explicit, never assumed.
  2. 2
    Origin stays visiblePublic posts show where the relationship or contribution began.
  3. 3
    Public value can travelHelpful posts, reels, resources, and wins strengthen the shared feed.
  4. 4
    Private stays privateMessages and limited-audience content never cross their chosen boundary.
