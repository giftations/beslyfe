# Beslyfe roadmap

The active product roadmap is maintained in [`docs/BESLYFE_ROADMAP.md`](docs/BESLYFE_ROADMAP.md).

The product order is deliberate:

1. One trustworthy identity and shared community.
2. Guided creation without unnecessary modules.
3. A clear sales or growth action for every build.
4. Measurement, automation, and accountable bots.
5. A marketplace and recommendation layer only after trust and outcome data are strong.

The original event implementation is proof, not the default product shape. Its private data remains isolated; approved public community contributions can join the wider Beslyfe network with their origin shown.
