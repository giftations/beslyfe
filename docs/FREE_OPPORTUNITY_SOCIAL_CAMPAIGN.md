# Beslyfe Free Opportunity Campaign

Status: active production campaign beginning July 18, 2026.

## Promise

Beslyfe is a 100% free place to turn a hope, skill, business idea, creative practice, community, event, or new kind of project into a practical system. The campaign shows a believable first step, supportive people, and meaningful progress. It never promises guaranteed wealth or instant success.

## Creative rules

- Never publish member, user, follower, like, or view totals in campaign posts, reels, Stories, or artwork.
- Keep imagery classy, futuristic, and comforting: forest green, cream, teal, restrained warm gold, human warmth, and subtle connected light.
- Show dignity, attainable progress, collaboration, useful work, and fulfilled purpose.
- Avoid money piles, luxury cars, influencer glamour, cold cyberpunk neon, fake testimonials, exaggerated claims, and cannabis imagery.
- Say that Beslyfe is 100% free. Do not imply that every outside provider, payment processor, ad network, or third-party service is free.
- Every call to action links to Beslyfe with campaign attribution and no personal data.
