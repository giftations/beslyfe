# Erie Builders campaign

## Goal and audience

The campaign is designed for Erie-area first-time entrepreneurs, side-hustle builders, and small-business owners who need more income but may have little startup money, no technical confidence, or no clear idea yet. Its internal stopping condition is 100 verified, approved, non-admin Beslyfe accounts. The member count never appears in public copy.

The promise is intentionally practical: Beslyfe is 100% free, starts with questions, saves the member's work, helps compare realistic options, and connects the member to a community. It does not promise quick money or guaranteed results.

## Three daily posts

The scheduler publishes one distinct post in each America/New_York window:

1. Morning diagnosis: meet “I don't know,” “I need money,” and “whatever it takes” with useful questions and a realistic first step.
2. Midday action: teach one small demand, offer, customer, or system exercise the reader can use immediately.
3. Evening community: invite honest progress, questions, lessons, and peer support without hype.

Every feed campaign also creates separate Facebook and Instagram Story deliveries. All links open Beslyfe's question-first entry and use `utm_campaign=erie_builder_100`, with date, slot, and tested time in `utm_content`.

## Facebook community outreach

Third-party Facebook groups are partnership channels, not broadcast inventory. Beslyfe must inspect each group's current rules and obtain moderator approval when promotion or resource links require it. It must not post three times a day into the same group, evade moderation, automate personal-profile behavior, or scrape members.

The first candidate should be a visible, active community for small-business owners and entrepreneurs whose rules expressly permit free resources or admin-approved partner posts. Outreach should lead with a useful question and free help, clearly disclose that the poster represents Beslyfe, and ask the moderator before including the link if the rules are unclear.

Suggested moderator note:

> Hi—I'm helping launch Beslyfe, a 100% free question-first platform and peer community for people trying to start or grow something without a clear plan or large budget. We would like to offer your members a practical “find your first viable offer” exercise and optional free workspace. Is one transparent resource post allowed here? We will follow your format, will not mass-message members, and will not repeat the post without approval.

## Measurement and stop condition

Admin analytics are the source of truth for campaign visits and verified signups. Social delivery state is the source of truth for channel success, failure, Story delivery, and idempotency. The scheduler queries the account database before every run and omits this campaign after the verified-member goal is reached. Manual admin publishing is blocked at the same stopping condition.

Monitor:

- verified members and remaining distance to goal;
- attributed visits for `erie_builder_100`;
- question-flow starts, account creation, email verification, and onboarding completion;
- per-channel feed and Story delivery;
- conversion by date, slot, and time;
- comments and recurring user objections that should improve future copy or onboarding.

Do not spend money, buy followers, create fake accounts, manufacture testimonials, claim unverified results, or post in a third-party group against its rules.
