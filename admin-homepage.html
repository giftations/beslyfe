Quick Actions

Applications

Vendor, sponsor and speaker submissions. Approve or reject each one — decisions are saved for the whole site.

Page Text

Edit every heading, label and paragraph on the selected page. Leave a field blank to keep the default wording.

Buttons & Links

Rename any button, change where it goes, set its colors, or hide it entirely. Leave label/link blank to keep the default.

Navbar Brand

Control the brand area in the navigation. Use text, an uploaded image URL from the Media Library, or both.

Package Purchase Links (Eventbrite)

Paste the Eventbrite links that let vendors and sponsors buy their packages. These links are only revealed to applicants you have approved — when an approved vendor or sponsor enters their email on the package purchase page, the matching link unlocks. Leave a field blank to keep that package locked.

The password that unlocks your password-protected Eventbrite ticket pages. It is sent to each applicant you approve and shown to them on the package purchase page. Leave blank to omit it.

Background & Theme

Control the hero background image (chosen in Media below), how dark the overlay is, where the image sits, and the brand colors.

Premade Templates

One click applies a coordinated color theme and overlay to the homepage. You can fine-tune afterwards in Background & Theme.

Content Settings

Section notes and toggles. Clearing a section's box hides that section on the homepage.

Homepage Section Order

Drag sections to reorder them on the homepage. Turn on Transparent for any section that should let the background show through.

    Media Library

    Upload images or videos and apply one as the background for the selected page. Photos (including ones straight from your phone) are automatically resized and optimized, and videos are auto-configured to stream on the site, so any upload just works. Media and your chosen background are saved on the site and published for every visitor.
