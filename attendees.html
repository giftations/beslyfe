Venue Information

Bayfront Convention Center – Erie, Pennsylvania

Parking

Parking is available in the Bayfront Convention Center's attached garage, with additional public lots and metered street parking throughout downtown Erie within a short walk of the venue. Arrive early on event day, as nearby spaces fill quickly.

Hotels & Lodging

Several hotels sit within walking distance of the Bayfront Convention Center, including the Sheraton Erie Bayfront Hotel and the Courtyard by Marriott Erie Bayfront. Additional options are available throughout downtown Erie for guests who prefer to stay nearby.

FAQ

What is the age policy? Bak'd On The Bay is a 21+ event. Everyone must be at least 21 years old to attend.

What do I need to get in? Bring your ticket (digital or printed) and a valid government-issued photo ID showing you are 21 or older. IDs will be checked at the door.

Is re-entry allowed? Yes — keep your wristband on and your ticket handy so you can come and go throughout the event.
